ABOUT

Mili Golas officially established in 2019 has become an authorized distributor of Fluidra, the top company in swimming pool and wellness industry around the world.

The meaning of words “Mili Golas” originally came from Javanese and Spanish,. Javanese represent the land where Mili Golas originally established and Spanish represent the Mili Golas first business partner Fluidra that originally from Spain.

In Javanes Mili Golas is the short for “mili saking tahun songolas”, means Mili Golas been flowing since 2019. While in Spanish Mili Golas Golas is the short for “Milones de Golas” - means millions goals. Mili Golas means that we have been established from 2019 and we have millions goals that we will achieve.
